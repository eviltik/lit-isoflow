lit-isoflow

Viewer and editor for isometric infrastructure diagrams, as a single Lit web component — no React.

1 Editor

The full component: place icons, draw connectors and zones, edit properties, undo/redo, export to SVG or PNG. Light and dark themes, English and French.

2 Stress test

How far does it go? Generate up to 10 000 nodes and measure what it costs — first render, frame rate while panning, DOM size. The numbers are what they are.
